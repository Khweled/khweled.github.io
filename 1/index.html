Images of the Russian Empire: Colorizing the Prokudin-Gorskii photo collection

This projects goal is to align the red, green, and blue color channels of images. The images that I have used were taken in early 1900s Russia. These photos were taken on black and white film but with a red, green and blue filter over the camera in order to only capture the light of that color. This was achived by taking three exposures, one ontop of another, to later be laid one ontop of another for true color images. Due to this procedure the photos are not always 100% aligned. Here I will describe my procedure for automatically aligning these images.
